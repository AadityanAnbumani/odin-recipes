Chicken Alfredo

Description:

Chicken Alfredo is a rich and creamy pasta that is sue to captivate both friends and family alike. It is a pretty simple recipe that you and your loved ones can enjoy all year round.

You can also make Chicken Alfredo with all different kinds of meats like shrimp, bacon, and even mushrooms.I hope you enjoy this recipe that is sure to put a smile on everyone's face.

Ingredients:

  • 16 ounces of dry fetuccine pasta
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Parmegiano Reggiano cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the fetuccini based on the packagin instructions and save some of the pasta water once it is done cooking
  2. In a pan on low heat, add the butter
  3. Once the butter is melted add the cooked pasta
  4. Once the pasta is coated in the butter add some the pasta water
  5. Sprinkle in the parmegiano reggiano cheese and cook until the cheese is melted
  6. To finish it off add some more parmegiano reggiano on top once the pasta is done cooking
